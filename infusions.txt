Basic recipe:
Similar to making tea, but it is also typically left to stand for longer then when making tea (often 10 to 20 minutes)

Crataegus monogyna/laevigata (calming):
1 to 2 coffee spoons (=1 to 1,5 grams) of ? per cup of hot water
Leave to stand for 20 minutes.
Strain.
3 cups per day
Recipe based on: Groot Handboek Geneeskrachtige Planten by Geert Verhelst


Melissa officinale (calming):
2 to 3 coffee spoons (=1,5 to 4,5 grams) of leaves (folia) per cup of hot water
Leave to stand for 15 minutes.
Strain.
3 to 5 cups per day

Tilia cordata (calming):

1 cup per day
